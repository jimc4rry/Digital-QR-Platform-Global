{% trans "How to Create a QR Code Menu for Your Restaurant" %}

{% trans "You don't need a designer, a developer, or an app. Here's exactly how to go from a printed menu to a scannable digital one in about five minutes." %}

{% trans "1. Create an account" %}

{% blocktrans %}Sign up with your business name, email, and a password. A restaurant profile is created for you automatically the moment you sign up, along with a 30-day free trial - no credit card required to start.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "2. Organize your menu into categories" %}

{% blocktrans %}Before adding individual dishes, set up your categories - Starters, Mains, Drinks, Desserts, or whatever structure fits your menu. You can reorder them anytime, and customers see them exactly as you arrange them.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "3. Add your products" %}

{% blocktrans %}For each dish or drink, add a name, price, and a short description. You can attach a photo, mark items as vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, or spicy, and add optional extras like "add cheese" with their own price adjustment.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "4. Your QR code is generated automatically" %}

{% blocktrans %}As soon as your account is created, a unique QR code is generated for your menu - no design tool or third-party QR generator needed. If you take table-side orders, each table or sunbed can also get its own QR code that pins the order to that exact spot.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "5. Print it once, use it everywhere" %}

{% blocktrans %}Print the QR code on a table tent, a sticker at the entrance, or a small sign at the bar. Customers scan it with their phone's camera - no app to download - and the menu opens instantly in their browser.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "6. Update your menu anytime, for free" %}

{% blocktrans %}This is the real advantage over a printed menu: when a price changes, an item sells out, or you add a new seasonal dish, you update it from your dashboard and the change is live immediately. No reprinting, no waiting, no extra cost.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "What about taking orders?" %}

{% blocktrans %}If you want customers to order directly from their phone, table-side ordering can be turned on so each QR code is tied to a specific table. Orders land on a live dashboard your staff can manage in real time, with no extra hardware required.{% endblocktrans %}
